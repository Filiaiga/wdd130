Texas

Texas is in the Southern North American across the Gulf of Mexico. It is close to Louisiana, Arkansas, Oklahoma, and New Mexico. It is known as the Lone Star State because the Republic of Texas declared itself as an independent nation. Because of this, the Texas flag has a single star also known as the lone star.
